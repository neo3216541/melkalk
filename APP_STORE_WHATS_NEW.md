## Tips for App Store Submission

1. **Keep it under 4000 characters** (App Store limit)
2. **Use bullet points** for better readability
3. **Be honest** about ads - users appreciate transparency
4. **Highlight benefits** not just features
5. **Include call-to-action** (e.g., "Please rate us")
6. **Avoid technical jargon** unless your audience is technical
7. **Update for each version** - don't reuse old text
