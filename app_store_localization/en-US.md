WHAT IS MEL

The Minimum Equipment List (MEL) is an ICAO regulatory framework that defines which aircraft equipment can be inoperative while still allowing safe operations. The MEL Calculator helps you track compliance deadlines for four categories:

Category A: Shortest delay (typically 3 days)
Category B: Short delay (typically 10 days)
Category C: Medium delay (typically 30 days)
Category D: Longest delay (typically 120 days)
